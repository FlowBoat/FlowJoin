Science, Tech and Engineering Publication

First and foremost, FlowBoat functions as a writing publication. That means no matter what new toys we are playing with today, you can always count on us to get out exciting new stories.

Cool Toys and a Workspace for Innovation Projects

If you're an ambitious young (high school) student with great ideas, but don't know where to start, FlowBoat HQ is the place for you. We provide advice and infrastructure for students who want to start making cool things now.

Bigger things! "Like Really Big"

We work together with the University of Waterloo to connect the exceptional youth of tomorrow with the brightest minds of today. If you need resources or ideas for projects, we can hook you up!

Where does it all happen?

Currently FlowBoat is under consideration for a Google Non-Profit Grant, which would give us the opportunity to lease an office space at the Boardwalk, Waterloo, ON. But unless we can prove to Google that we are worth their investment, we won't get the opportunity of a lifetime.

Major Divisions of FlowBoat

Like a ship on its maiden voyage, FlowBoat needs a dedicated crew to set its sails.
Since FlowBoat is a multidisciplinary non-profit organization, we are sure there will be a place for you onboard.

  • FlowLead

    FlowBoat has many operations that are all different from one another in execution, timeline and objectives. Therefore, it is imperative that we have strong leaders guiding our workflow.

  • FlowNews

    Running a youth think tank is very costly. That's why the most important branch is FlowNews, our primary revenue stream. If you're a talented writer, FlowNews is right for you.

  • FlowTech

    FlowBoat is first and foremost a think tank. If you've got an idea- or spark- but don't know where to go with it, that's where we come in. We'll work with you to make it.

  • FlowThink

    We're always trying to break the universe. Doing research and coming up with the next big thing is what we do best. If you're strong in mathematics and science, join us.

  • FlowMedia

    What good would it be to run a think tank and not share our findings with the world? FlowMedia does just that. If you're a skilled videographer and socialite, come aboard.

  • FlowMoney

    FlowBoat is not all fun and games. On break from all the fun we need a serious and responsible team to handle FlowBoat's finances and its legal department.
