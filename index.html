Science and Engineering Publication

First and foremost, FlowBoat functions as a writing publication. That means no matter what new toys we are playing with today, you can always count on us to get out exciting new stories.

Workspace for Innovation Projects

If you're an ambitious highschool student with great ideas, but don't know where to start. FlowBoat HQ is the place for you. We provide consultancy and support for students who want to start making cool things now.

Bigger things!

We work together with the University of Waterloo to the connect exceptional youth of tomorrow with the bright minds of today. If you need resources or ideas for projects, we can hook you up!

The Grant

Currently FlowBoat is under consideration for a Google Non-Profit Grant! which makes it a serious possibility that we'd have the ability to lease an office space at the Boardwalk, Waterloo, ON. But unless we can prove to Google that we can function autonomously we won't have the oppertunity of a life time.
